Features

Everything you need to
manage your inventory

A fast, native desktop app that makes working with CS2 storage units painless.

Bulk Operations

Move multiple items to or from storage units at once. No more one-by-one clicking.

Item Pricing

See the total value of your inventory and each storage unit at a glance.

Multi-Account

Save and switch between multiple Steam accounts with ease.

Item Grouping

Items are grouped by type with collapsible sections for a clean overview.

Search & Filter

Quickly find items across your inventory and storage units.

Cross Platform

Available on Windows, macOS, and Linux. Native desktop experience.
